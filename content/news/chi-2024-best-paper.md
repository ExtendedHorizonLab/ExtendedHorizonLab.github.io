We are thrilled to announce that our paper "SoundScape VR: Audio-Based Navigation for Blind Users in Virtual Reality" has received the **Best Paper Award** at CHI 2024!

This recognition highlights the importance of accessibility research in virtual reality and the dedication of our team to making VR experiences inclusive for everyone.
