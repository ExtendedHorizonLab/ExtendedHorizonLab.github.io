# Join Our Lab

We are always looking for talented and motivated researchers to join our team!

## PhD Students

We recruit PhD students through the university's graduate admissions process. If you're interested in working with us:

1. Apply to the PhD program in Computer Science
2. Mention your interest in our lab in your application
3. Reach out to Prof. John Doe after submitting your application

**What we look for:**
- Strong background in computer science, HCI, or related fields
- Research experience (publications are a plus but not required)
- Passion for accessibility, urban computing, or human-AI interaction
- Strong communication and collaboration skills

## Masters Students

Masters students interested in research should:
- Take a course with Prof. John Doe
- Reach out with a specific research idea or project of interest
- Be prepared to commit at least 2 quarters to research

## Undergraduate Students

We welcome motivated undergraduates who want to gain research experience:

**Requirements:**
- At least sophomore standing
- Completed core CS courses (CSE 14x, 33x)
- Ability to commit 10+ hours per week
- Interest in our research areas

**How to apply:**
1. Review our current projects
2. Send an email to Prof. John Doe with:
   - Your resume/CV
   - Unofficial transcript
   - A brief statement about which project interests you and why

## Visiting Researchers

We occasionally host visiting researchers and postdocs. Please reach out directly with:
- Your CV
- A description of your research and how it connects to ours
- Proposed visit duration and timing
- Funding situation

## Contact

For any questions about joining the lab, please email: lab@university.edu

We look forward to hearing from you!
